MediCitas is a Laravel-based medical appointment management system that gives clinics a structured way to coordinate patient visits. Patients can book appointments with specific doctors, view their history, and cancel upcoming visits. Doctors see their incoming schedule, manage appointment details, and mark visits as complete.

## What MediCitas does

MediCitas addresses the core scheduling needs of a medical clinic:

- **Dual-role access** — Patients and doctors log in separately with their own account types. Each sees only data relevant to their role.
- **Appointment lifecycle** — Every appointment moves through three states: `programada` (scheduled), `cancelada` (cancelled), and `completada` (completed).
- **Schedule conflict prevention** — The system validates that a doctor cannot have two appointments at the same date and time.
- **Daily dashboard** — After login, both patients and doctors land on a dashboard showing today's appointments at a glance.
- **Appointment history** — All past appointments remain accessible for both user types.

## Core entities

| Entity | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Paciente** (Patient) | A registered patient who can book appointments |
| **Medico** (Doctor) | A licensed physician who receives appointment requests |
| **Cita** (Appointment) | A scheduled meeting between a patient and doctor at a specific date/time |

## Tech stack

MediCitas is built on:

- **Laravel 13** — PHP web framework with Eloquent ORM
- **SQLite** (default) or MySQL/PostgreSQL — relational database
- **Tailwind CSS 4 + Vite** — frontend styling and asset bundling
- **Laravel session guards** — separate authentication contexts for patients and doctors
